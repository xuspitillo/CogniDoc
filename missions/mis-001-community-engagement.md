## Objective [*(BIZ.W1.DEF>S1)*]

Drive adoption of CogniDoc Tier 1 by engaging early adopters, recruiting design partners, and refining the public-facing material based on real-world feedback.

The goal of this mission is **literacy and validation**: prove that the four protocols (SBS, PSS, CPA, Classification) are learnable in under an hour by a developer with no prior context, and that they deliver perceptible value within a week of adoption.

### Specific objectives

1. Identify and onboard 10 design partners across at least 3 different domains
2. Gather structured feedback covering 3 documented use cases (knowledge base, research notes, software project)
3. Refine specs, templates, and getting-started docs based on synthesized feedback
