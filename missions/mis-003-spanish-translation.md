## Objective [*(COG.W2.DEF>S1)*]

Produce a complete, idiomatic Spanish translation of the public CogniDoc Tier 1 release: specifications, conceptual documentation, and templates.

This is a **SATELLITE** mission — independent of the main product roadmap. It activates only when community signals indicate sustained interest from non-English-speaking adopters.

### Specific objectives

1. Translate the four protocol specifications (SBS, PSS, CPA, Classification) into Spanish
2. Translate the conceptual documentation (getting-started, concepts, comparison, glossary, upgrade-path, use-cases)
3. Translate the templates (MEMORY, SIGNAL_REGISTRY, missions, handshakes) preserving structural fidelity
